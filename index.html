My Senior Project

For my senior project, I initially wanted to fully design and 3D Print a mini piano. However, after talking with my mentors, I realized that idea was unfeasible. Instead, I decided to import a design for a 3D Printed recorder and modify the design so that the recorder sounded like a handmade version.
